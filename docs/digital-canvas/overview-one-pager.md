# Digital Canvas

**A San Antonio builder program connecting AI-native talent to industry pain points and the capital that funds them.**

---

## What it is

Digital Canvas is a vertical-focused, cohort-based program for San Antonio builders. Each cohort opens with an industry-themed workshop authored by a corporate underwriter, runs through a six-week build bridge out of DevSA's downtown coworking space, and ends with a demo day in front of the Alamo Angels investor network and Texas capital partners.

We are not an accelerator. We do not take equity. We do not write checks. We connect builders who can ship with AI tools to industries that need solutions and to the capital that funds them.

---

## The model

```
  Workshop weekend     →    Six-week bridge     →    Demo day
  ─────────────────         ───────────────         ────────────
  Industry keynote          DevSA coworking         Alamo Angels
  Pain points framed        Sponsor office hour     network + partners
  AI tools, prompt          Pitch coaching          Curated pitches
  to production             Investor mixer          Warm follow-ups
  Working prototype         Dress rehearsal
  by Sunday night
```

Each cohort is themed to one San Antonio-relevant vertical: cybersecurity, military, health and science, education, or adjacent.

## Why San Antonio

The verticals national accelerators under-serve — defense, cyber, military health, regulated industries — are the verticals SA leads in. JBSA, 16th Air Force, UT Health, BAMC, USAA, and a deep ecosystem of contractors and operators are headquartered or anchored here. Digital Canvas builds against that, not against it.

---

## Why now

AI tooling has collapsed the gap between "I have an idea" and "I have a working demo" from weeks to hours. A free workshop weekend can produce credible prototypes. A six-week bridge can turn prototypes into pitches. The bottleneck is no longer build velocity — it is connecting builders to the right pain points and the right rooms.

---

## How to engage

- **Builders:** Apply to a cohort. Open calls are shared across DevSA and Digital Canvas social channels, and amplified by ecosystem partners (Alamo Angels, Geekdom, Tech Bloc, Bexar County ECD).
- **Underwriters:** Sponsor a vertical. Get named exclusivity, talent pipeline, and first-look access.
- **Investors:** Attend demo day. Pre-curated dealflow, no sourcing cost.
- **Ecosystem partners:** Co-promote, refer builders, host adjacent programming.
