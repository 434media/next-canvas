# Digital Canvas — Underwriter Brief

**A localized, vertical-focused builder program putting San Antonio talent in front of capital. Underwrite a cohort. Own the vertical.**

---

## The opportunity

San Antonio's economy runs on cybersecurity, military, health and science, and education. The talent to build in these verticals already lives here — but the path from idea to investor has been fragmented. Digital Canvas is the connecting tissue: an AI-native builder program that runs themed cohorts against real industry pain points, ending in a curated demo day in front of Alamo Angels' 140+ accredited investors and the broader Texas capital network (Capital Factory, Venture STX, Bexar County).

Underwriting a cohort makes your company the named industry partner for that vertical — the source of the painpoints, the first audience for the solutions, and the first hand raised when something is worth a deeper conversation.

---

## What you get

- **Named vertical exclusivity** for the cohort window (e.g., "Cybersecurity Cohort, powered by [Underwriter]")
- **Painpoint authorship** — your team frames the keynote and the problem set the builders solve against
- **First-look access** to every builder and prototype before demo day
- **Talent pipeline** — direct line to builders demonstrating skill against your problems
- **Investor-grade media** — branded recap content, builder profiles, and demo day footage produced by 434 Media
- **Demo day presence** — sponsor recognition in front of the Alamo Angels network and partner investors
- **Office hour residency** — your domain experts host one session with the cohort (high-signal, low-lift)

---

## How a cohort runs

1. **Workshop weekend** — DevSA hosts a free, industry-themed build weekend. Your keynote opens with the pain points. Builders use AI tooling (Cursor, Claude, Codex, Gemini) to ship working prototypes in a prompt-to-production format.
2. **Six-week bridge** — committed builders work out of the DevSA downtown coworking space. Touchpoints include a sponsor office hour (you), pitch coaching from Alamo Angels staff, peer review, and an investor mixer.
3. **Demo day** — curated pitches to the Alamo Angels network and partner investors. Warm conversations continue organically.

---

## What we ask

A single underwriting commitment per cohort. Covers space, mentorship coordination, demo day production, and media deliverables. Pricing and term sheet shared on request — sized to a corporate marketing or innovation budget, not a venture allocation.

---

## Why now

- AI tooling has collapsed the build cycle — workshop-week prototypes are demo-day credible
- San Antonio's vertical strengths are under-served by national accelerators that default to consumer/SaaS
- Alamo Angels and DevSA already operate the capital and community infrastructure — Digital Canvas is the program layer on top, not net-new infrastructure to stand up
