# Digital Canvas — Build Something Real. Pitch Real Investors.

**A free, AI-native builder cohort for San Antonio. Six weeks. One demo day. 140+ accredited investors in the room.**

---

## The pitch

You can already build with AI tools faster than you ever could alone. Digital Canvas turns that into something investors can write checks against.

We run themed cohorts against real industry pain points — cybersecurity, military, health and science — authored by the companies that live those problems. You build. We coach. You demo to the Alamo Angels network and Texas capital partners. Conversations continue from there if there's a fit.

No equity. No tuition. No catch. We ask you to commit to the demo day.

---

## What you'll do

**Workshop weekend.** Industry keynote opens with the real pain points. You build with the AI tools you already use — Cursor, Claude, Codex, Gemini — in a prompt-to-production format. Ship a working prototype by Sunday night.

**Six-week bridge.** Work out of DevSA's downtown coworking space alongside the cohort. Tap into:
- Office hour with the underwriter's domain experts
- Pitch coaching from Alamo Angels staff
- Peer review with other cohort builders
- Investor mixer — meet the room before demo day
- Dress rehearsal with feedback before you go live

**Demo day.** Pitch the Alamo Angels network and partner investors. Curated audience. Warm room. Real follow-up conversations.

## How to join

### Step 1 — RSVP to the workshop

Workshops are open. Light intake: name, contact, a few words on what brings you. No idea required yet — you'll get one from the keynote.

Open calls are shared across DevSA and Digital Canvas social channels, and amplified by ecosystem partners (Alamo Angels, Geekdom, Tech Bloc, Bexar County ECD).

### Step 2 — Commit to the cohort

By Monday morning after the workshop weekend, apply to continue into the six-week bridge and demo day. Three short answers:

1. Which of the keynote painpoints are you tackling, and how?
2. Why you, why now?
3. What would a working demo look like in six weeks?

That's the whole application. We review same-week and confirm cohort spots.
